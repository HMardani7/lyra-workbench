## License Key
- ✅ **CC0** — public domain, sell freely, no attribution needed
- ✅ **CC BY** — commercial OK, credit the designer
- ⚠️ **CC BY-SA** — commercial OK but remixes must stay CC BY-SA
- ⚠️ **CC BY-ND** — commercial OK but no modifications
- ✅ **Commercial No-Deriv** — paid models, sell prints as-is
- 🚫 **CC BY-NC / Standard Digital File** — REJECTED
