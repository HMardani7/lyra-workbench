Research date: 2026-06-14  
Prepared for: Hammad / Lyra workbench  
Goal: find STL/3MF/OBJ files that can be used for an Etsy 3D-print store, prioritising **free + commercial-use allowed**, with low-cost paid files acceptable where the license clearly allows sale of printed physical items.

## Important license/IP rules

- **STL license and franchise IP are separate.** A designer can allow sale of printed physical items, but Marvel/DC/Disney/Sony/anime IP can still trigger Etsy takedowns.
- **Commercial-use OK candidates:** CC0, CC BY, CC BY-SA, Cults CU, Cults CU-ND, or explicit seller text saying physical printed products may be sold.
- **Reject/avoid:** CC BY-NC, CC BY-NC-SA, CC BY-NC-ND, Cults PU/private use, “personal use only,” MakerWorld Standard Digital File License, MyMiniFactory Standard Digital File Store License, or CGTrader Royalty Free unless the seller explicitly grants sale of physical prints.
- **For fan-art props:** avoid exact franchise names, character names, official logos, and trademark phrases in Etsy titles/tags. This reduces risk but does not eliminate it.
- **Before buying paid files:** screenshot/save the page showing commercial permission and keep the receipt. If terms are unclear, message the designer for written permission.
